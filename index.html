Family History

Growing up my Mother Mama FeFe would cook for family gatherings. She would prepare meals for Christmas, Thanksgiving, New Year's, birthday parties, church functions, and many other events. Mama FeFe even catered my wedding reception, and the food was delicious. Our family migrated from the deep south. They travelled from Arkansas and Lousiana to San Francisco. Mama FeFe learn from her Mother and Grandmother on how to cook and to use spices in recipes. She would cook different types of American Cusines. Mama FeFe can cook Louisana Creole, Soul Food, and Low Country cusine.
