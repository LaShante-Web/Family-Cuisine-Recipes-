Family Cuisine Recipes

My family migrated from the deep south to San Francisco. My mother learned how to cook from her mother and grandmother. Growing up my mother would cook for family gatherings. She would prepare meals for Christmas, Thanksgiving, New Year's, birthday parties, church functions, and other events. My Mother catered my wedding reception, and the food was delicious. Living in San Francisco, my mother and I learned how to cook different types of American cuisine, ethnic cuisines, and incorporate different spices. My family specialities are: Louisana Creole Cuisine, Soul Food, Gullah-Geechee Cusine. My mother taught me how to cook. I will share some easy recipes.
