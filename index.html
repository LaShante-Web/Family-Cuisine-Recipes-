Family Cuisine and Recipes

Family

Growing up my Mother would cook for family gatherings. She would prepare meals for Christmas, Thanksgiving, New Year's, birthday parties, church functions, and many other events. My Mothereven catered my wedding reception, and the food was delicious. Our family migrated from the deep south. They travelled from Arkansas and Lousiana to San Francisco. My Mother learned how to cook from her Mother and Grandmother on how to cook and to use spices in recipes. She would cook different types of American Cusines. My Mother can cook Louisana Creole Cuisine, Soul Food, Gullah-Geechee Cusine, and other ethnic cuisines. I will share some of my family favorite recipes
